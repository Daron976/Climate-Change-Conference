United Nations Climate Change Conference 2022

Global climate discussions to bring forth ideas, innovations and objective analysis through creativity and critical thinking to trigger affirmative action to protect our planet, for this is home.

2022.11.6(Sun) ~ 18(Fri)

@ Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt

Main Program

World leaders Summit

World Leaders Summit will highlight the critical issues of this unique moment in time to build a more prosperous future.

Finance

Finance is the cornerstone for implementing climate actions and scaling up ambition

Science & Youth

Several landmark science reports from IPCC, UNEP, and other institutions, ensuring that the voice of Youth and Future Generations is heard

Decarbonization

Several energy intensive sectors and companies have come forward with plans and policies and actions aiming to reduce their carbon footprints

Agriculture & Adaptation

Adaptation and Resilience are of crucial importance to all parties and in particular developing countries

Water & Gender

The role of women in dealing with all aspects of the climate change challenge is central, crucial and indispensable. Water is the source of life and livelihoods

Energy & ACE

Climate action requires engagement with, and contributions from all stakeholders, The Energy Day would deal with all aspects of energy and climate change

Biodiversity

The impacts of climate change on biodiversity and the means to mobilize the global actions towards the challenges to halt biodiversity loss and to reduce the impacts of climate change and pollution

Solutions

Possible solutions from the holistic, cross cutting solutions such as greening of national budgets, multilevel action and sustainable transport, to sectoral solutions like waste management
